Glossary

The Textile & Materials Glossary and The Persistence of Color Glossary provide English translations of French textile, material, and color terms. These prototypes are the first of their kind. They stand to give public access to eighteenth and early nineteenth-century words which have since often altered in definition or have been totally forgotten. Each of the glossaries further expose the importance of accurate description to the fashion plates published in the Journal des dames et des modes.
